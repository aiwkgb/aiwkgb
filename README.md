# Muhammad Fajar Mauludi

**Personal AI Office Builder for non-technical founders.**

I build public-safe AI workflows, templates, and lightweight operating systems that help low-resource builders turn scattered ideas into clearer documents, dashboards, roadmaps, and executable next actions.

I am a generalist from Indonesia: part creative strategist, part AI operator, part product/workflow builder. My current focus is not to pretend every beginner needs a complex tech stack. ## Responsible AI and privacy stance

My public repositories use demo data and public-safe examples.

I avoid publishing:

- secrets, tokens, API keys, and `.env` files,
- client/customer private data,
- finance, legal, family, or personal sensitive data,
- private operational screenshots,
- real account IDs or private lead lists.

The goal is to show useful AI workflows without exposing private operational reality.
